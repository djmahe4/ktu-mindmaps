```
Network Devices
|
|-- Active Hub
|   |-- Operates at the physical layer
|   |-- Connects multiple devices together
|   |-- Strengthens the signal
|   |-- Needs electricity to operate
|   |-- Acts as a multi-point repeater
|
|-- Switch
    |-- Operates at the data link layer
    |-- Enables connection establishment and termination
    |-- Makes filtering decisions based on MAC address
    |-- Can have a buffer to hold frames for processing
    |-- Can forward frames faster
```
