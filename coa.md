| Mechanism           | CPU Involvement     | Speed     | Use Case                |
|---------------------|---------------------|-----------|-------------------------|
| Programmed I/O      | High (polling)      | Slow      | Small, slow devices     |
| Interrupt-Driven I/O| Medium (ISR)        | Moderate  | General I/O (e.g., disk)|
| DMA                 | Low (setup only)    | Fast      | Large transfers         |
